WeRateDogs THREE Insights in full¶

Data gotten from the real-world data is rarely clean. Using python and its libraries, we have gathered over 5000 tweet data as they stood on 1st of August 2017 of Twitter user @dog_rates, also known as WeRateDogs, from various sources and in various formats, assessed its quality and tidiness and cleaned it.

WeRateDogs is a Twitter account with over 4 million followers that rates people's dogs. These ratings almost always have a denominator of 10 albeit the numerators, almost always greater than 10 i.e. 11/10, 12/10, 13/10, etc.

Herein we discuss the observation from three important insights about the WeRateDogs data before the 1st of August 2017.

Which dog breed is the most liked and how it compares in terms of retweet?¶

With over 50 different dog breeds in the WeRateDogs data and the plot below shows the number of times each dog breed was liked and retweeted the given timeframe.

The bar chart above shows each dog breed with their corresponding retweet and favorite counts. It clearly shows that golden retrievers are the most liked and most retweeted, followed by labrador retrievers and Chihuahua. No retweet count is greater than the favorite count for each dog specie.

Also, it's clear that dog specie with higher favorite counts tend to have more retweet count.

Which dog stage has the highest average rating?¶

According to Dogtionary, there are several stages of dog. However, because of the scope of the project we focused on just four dog stages i.e. Doggo, Floofer, Puppo and Pupper. The plot below shows the diffence in average numerator ratings of each dog stage.

The pie chart above clearly shows that less than one-quarter of each dog stage average rating is greater than the total mean. Although, the puppo dog stage is marginally higher than floofer, doggo and pupper, with 20.2%, 20.0% and 19.6 % respectively.

It also shows that more than 19% of the average rating greater than the total mean is neither puppo, floofer,doggo nor pupper.

Which dog stage is most liked and how it compares in terms of retweet?¶

The variant plots below represensts each dog stage by retweet and favorite count.

The charts above displays the dog stage average count by the Favorite and retweet column. CONCLUSIONS¶

Although, there's no clear correlation between retweet and favorite count, the plots above conveys a clear message regarding the questions posed, showing that the golden retrievers are the most liked and most retweeted, followed by labrador retrievers and Chihuahua and the puppo dog stage is marginally higher than the floofer, doggo and pupper dog stage in terms of the amount of average ratings greater or equal to the total average rating of approximately 1.09.

It also shows that despite the (i.e. pupper) dog stage having the least amount of average ratings that is greater or equal to the total average rating; 19.6%, it has more favorite count but lesser retweet count than floofer with more average ratings, 20.2%.
